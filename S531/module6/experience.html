Work Experience

Ajayi's career is a testament to his versatility, adaptability, and unwavering dedication to his craft. From his early days as an instructor in Nigeria to his current pursuit of higher education in Finland, he has navigated the ever-changing landscape of technology with grace and tenacity.

Throughout his career, Ajayi has remained committed to empowering others and sharing his knowledge with the next generation of technology enthusiasts. Whether through his work as an educator, mentor, or consultant, he strives to inspire, educate, and uplift those around him, leaving a lasting impact wherever he goes.
